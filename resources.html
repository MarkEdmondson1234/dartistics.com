Resources

Resource #1: Join the Measure Slack team at http://join.measure.chat. It’s free! Be sure to join the #r-and-statistics channel!

Resoure #2: Check out the resources below. This is a community-driven set of resources and links on R and statistics generated of, by, and for digital analysts. To submit a resource, fill out this Google form. Once approved, the resource will appear here (through the magic of the googlesheets package and some RMarkdown!). (We are continuing to work on the structure and navigation.)
